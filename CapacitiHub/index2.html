More About Us

Established in 2010, CAPACITI, part of the UVU Africa Group, is at the forefront of preparing young individuals for the tech sector. CAPACITI has emerged as one of South Africa‚Äôs leading digital talent accelerators, annually training and placing approximately 1000 unemployed youth. CAPACITI is dedicated to fostering the inclusive growth of the digital economy by creating a pipeline of future-fit, skilled individuals equipped with relevant digital skills aligned with industry demand. Since its inception, CAPACITI has been refining solutions that aim to economically integrate talent, by providing scalable digital skills training and preparing them for the evolving workplace of the future. As a non-profit organisation, CAPACITI leverages partner investments to deliver demand-driven, targeted technical training that positively impacts lives.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a vision to bridge Africa's digital skills gap, CAPACITI has evolved into a transformational initiative for a continent-wide movement for digital empowerment.

We recognised that traditional education systems weren't keeping pace with the rapidly evolving digital economy. Our response? Proactive, industry-leading programmes that connect talent with opportunity.

Today, we're proud to have trained over 1,200 individuals, achieved an 87% placement rate, and partnered with leading organisations across the continent.
